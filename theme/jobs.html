To fulfill this enormously lofty task – baking different international breads on the land of Saints and Scholars; one would be exhausted, but not us.

We have found balance in the syncretism of three pillars that like pieces of a puzzle fit together and fortify each other and the entire Business.

These are:
* Latest in technology, equipment and techniques.
* Really brave and daring experiments with assortments of new produce.
AND
* Selection and recruitment of talented specialists.

If you are creative, daring, hard working, with a strong focus on quality and team work, have hands-on approach and interested in working with us, please send us your CV and see how it goes…

We would love to hear more about you and your talent!
